# Kerb Appeal That Protects Value: Jet Washing at Cheltenham House, Watford

Kerb appeal is not just for sellers. In a managed residential block, the condition of the communal areas shapes how every resident feels about their home, and how much a prospective buyer is willing to pay for it. At Cheltenham House on The Reeds in Watford, our **jet washing** restored the entrance, steps and path to give the whole block a noticeable lift in presentation. This case study was prepared for Ms Jennifer Mann, Senior Property Manager at Rendall & Rittner Ltd.

## Why Communal Presentation Drives Value

When a buyer or valuer walks up to a block, they form a judgement before they reach the front door. Dirty steps, stained paths and grimy entrances signal a building that is not well looked after, and that perception feeds directly into asking prices and saleability. Clean, well-maintained communal areas do the reverse. **Kerb appeal pressure washing** is one of the most cost-effective ways a managing agent can support property values across an entire development.

## The Work We Carried Out

We focused on the most visible shared surfaces: the **communal entrance, the steps and the approach path**. The job took a three-stage approach for a lasting finish:

1. **Manual scrubbing** to lift ingrained dirt and overgrown mould.
2. A **light chemical treatment** to tackle biological staining cleanly.
3. A **strong jet wash** to flush the surface back to its original condition.

## The Result

The before and after images show a clear uplift. The entrance and path at Cheltenham House were left brighter, cleaner and far more presentable, the kind of finish that lifts the look of the whole building and that residents enjoy through spring and summer.

## A Small Spend With Outsized Impact

For freeholders and managing agents, communal **exterior cleaning** delivers some of the best value of any maintenance line. It is highly visible, relatively low cost, and directly supports both resident satisfaction and property values. Building it into the annual maintenance plan keeps a development looking cared for year-round.

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Does pressure washing really affect property value?**
Presentation strongly influences buyer perception. Clean, well-maintained communal areas help a block show at its best and support stronger valuations.

**How often should communal areas be cleaned to maintain kerb appeal?**
An annual jet wash is the baseline for most blocks, with high-traffic or shaded areas benefiting from more frequent visits.

**Is jet washing safe for older surfaces?**
Yes. We adjust pressure and method to suit the surface, restoring it without causing damage.
