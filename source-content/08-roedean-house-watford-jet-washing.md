# Powerful Results, Gentle Methods: Eco-Friendly Jet Washing at Roedean House, Watford

You should not have to choose between a deep clean and a responsible one. At Roedean House on The Reeds in Watford, our team restored the communal areas using **eco-friendly jet washing**, proving that high-pressure cleaning and sustainable methods go hand in hand. This case study was prepared for Ms Jennifer Mann, Senior Property Manager at Rendall & Rittner Ltd.

## The Eco-First Approach

Many cleaning contractors lean heavily on strong chemicals to do the work. We take a different view. The bulk of the cleaning at Roedean House was done through physical methods, manual scrubbing and high-pressure water, with only a **light chemical treatment** used where biological growth genuinely needed it. That is the heart of **sustainable pressure washing**: let the method, not the chemistry, do the heavy lifting.

This matters in a communal setting. Run-off from over-chemicalled cleaning can harm surrounding planting, drains and the residents and pets who use the space. Minimising chemical use protects all three.

## The Work We Carried Out

We cleaned the **entrance, the steps and the path leading to them** in three stages:

1. **Manual scrubbing** to lift ingrained dirt and overgrown mould physically.
2. A **light, targeted treatment** applied only where needed, not blanket-sprayed.
3. A **strong jet wash** using high-pressure water to flush the surface clean.

## The Result

The before and after pictures show a significant uplift. The communal areas at Roedean House came up bright and clean, achieved with minimal chemical input, and ready for residents to enjoy through the spring and summer.

## Why Eco-Friendly Cleaning Is the Right Choice for Blocks

For managing agents and residents who care about sustainability, **green cleaning** is increasingly the standard, not the exception. Eco-conscious methods reduce harm to surrounding gardens and wildlife, are safer for residents and pets, and reflect well on a development's values. With the right technique, they deliver results every bit as strong as chemical-heavy alternatives.
